Introduction

1 - What is the solar eclipse?

astronomical event that occurs when an astronomical object or spacecraft is temporarily obscured..
This causes an eclipse of the Sun, or a solar eclipse. During a solar eclipse, the Moon casts a shadow onto Earth.

2 - What are the types of solar eclipse?

Can everyone watch the eclipse? Only a small area on Earth can watch it as a Total solar eclipse. There are then three types of eclipses:
